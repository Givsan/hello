Welcome to Traverse Computation Form

In traverse surveying, we use known reference points to calculate the precise coordinates of additional, unknown locations. This method involves measuring the distances and calculating bearings between established stations, then applying geometric and trigonometric principles to determine their exact positions. This computation form simplifies this process by allowing you to input your known coordinates, angles, and distances, and then it calculates the bearings, corrected angles, and coordinates of the unknown stations.
